# OpenCue 1.0 Quick Start

Welcome to OpenCue!

## Creating Your First Script
1. Open the app to see the Script Library.
2. Click **New Script** or click **Import** to bring in an existing `.md` or `.txt` file.
3. Edit the title and body of your script in the editor. OpenCue auto-saves locally!

## Prompting
1. Click the **Prompt** button to launch a distraction-free, floating prompter window.
2. Hover over the window to see controls.
3. Hit the **Spacebar** to play/pause.
4. Use the **Up/Down Arrows** to increase or decrease the scrolling speed multiplier.

## Adjusting Settings
1. Click the **Settings** gear icon in the editor toolbar to adjust typography.
2. Change the font size, line height, colors, and background opacity to your preference.
